Brewing Guide

Brew Every Blend Right

Water temperature and steep time make or break a cup. Here's how we brew each blend at Cha Khaben.

  • Masala Chai
    100°C, boiling 4–5 min

    Simmer with milk, whole spices, and a little sweetener for the boldest flavour. Never steep in lukewarm water.

  • Golden Tips
    85°C 2–3 min

    These delicate silver tips scorch easily — let boiled water rest for a minute before pouring, and never add milk.

  • Midnight Oolong
    90°C 3–4 min

    Good for up to 2 re-steeps — add 30 extra seconds each round to draw out the deeper chocolate notes.
